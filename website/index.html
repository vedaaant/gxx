How it works

01 — Watch Understands your screen A local watcher reacts to app switches and real changes, reads on-screen text, and only escalates to the vision model when it must.
02 — Remember Builds a private store Distilled summaries and embeddings live in an on-device store. Near-duplicates are dropped, so it stays small and yours.
03 — Answer Speaks when useful Ask by voice through Hermes, or let it surface something worth knowing — spoken back in your ElevenLabs voice.
